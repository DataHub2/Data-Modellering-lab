(- Conceptual Diagram model -) 

Person Inheritance:

The person entity centralizes the shared data beacuse all the entites bellow are "Persons".

. A Student is a Person.
. An Employee is a Person.
. A Consultant is a Person.
-------------------------------------------------
Role Assignments:

The roles of the staff based on employment status, this is so individuals can fullfill a specific educational function without data redundancy.

.An Employee can act as an Education Leader.
.An Employee can act as a Teacher.
.A Consultant can act as a Teacher.
-------------------------------------------------
Academic Structure and Organization:

The academic hierarchy links together classes, programs, locations, and relevant leaders to enable clear and adminstrativ resource allocation. 

. A Program consists of many Classes, but a specific Class belongs to only one Program.

. A Location hosts many Classes, but a specific Class is located at only one Location.

. An Education Leader manages many Classes, but a specific Class is managed by only one Education Leader.

. A Class contains many Students, but a specific Student belongs to only one Class.
-------------------------------------------------
Course Relations (Many to Many):

(M:M) relationsships gives us a dynamic where courses maybe shared across programs and taught be multiple teachers. 

. A Program includes many Courses, and a Course can be included in many Programs.

. A Teacher teaches many Courses, and a Course can be taught by many Teachers.
-------------------------------------------------
